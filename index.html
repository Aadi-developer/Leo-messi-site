Leo Messi

Introduction:
  1. Leo Messi is a world-class footballer with remarkable capabilities including playmaking, vision, dribbling, passing, and a high football IQ. He was born in Rosario, Argentina, in 1987.

  2. He scored his first goal against Albacete while playing for Barcelona, assisted by Ronaldinho. He made his debut in 2004.

  3. Currently, he plays for Inter Miami (MLS league in America). So far, he has scored 871 goals in his football career.
